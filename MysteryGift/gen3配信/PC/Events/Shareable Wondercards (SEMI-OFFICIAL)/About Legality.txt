NONE of the shareable Wonder Cards in this folder are official since, of course, the original cards were not shareable.

BUT when you share any of these Wonder Cards, the game that receives the card will have an EXACT COPY of the original, OFFICIAL event.

To sum up, these wondercard files are here for the solely purpose of injecting them into a savegame, then put that save on a game cartridge to use that game cartridge as a portable official Wonder Card pseudo-distribution device.
